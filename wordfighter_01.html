World Figter is a mobile game concept aimed at helping people learn new languages in a fun and enjoyable fashion. The game mixes regular platformer game levels with quick lessons. After slaying an enemy a lesson pops up and if you get the correct answer you can progress further. Currently being developed for english and hungarian.

Under production.
